### 3. Configure the Build
Fill in the following settings on the Render dashboard:
* **Name**: `univerge-platform`
* **Runtime**: `Python 3`
* [cite_start]**Build Command**: `pip install -r requirements.txt` [cite: 1]
* [cite_start]**Start Command**: `gunicorn app:app` [cite: 1]

3.  Add the following:
    * [cite_start]`DATABASE_URL`: (Paste your MongoDB connection string here) [cite: 1]
